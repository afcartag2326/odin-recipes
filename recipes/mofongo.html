Mofongo

Description

My personal favorite carribean dish. A true homage to my roots.

Ingredients

  • 4 Green Plantains
  • 1lb Chicharron
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 4 TSP Olive Oil
  • 2 Cups Canola Oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the plantains, cut them into 1.5 inch slices, soak them in salty cold-weather for 15 minutes, dran them and dry before putting on hot skillet with oil.
  2. Fry for about 12 minutes at medium-low heat or until they turn light brown. Make sure to turn them. Do not brown them too much, so they are easy to mash. Stick a fork in them to check if they are done.
  3. Remove plantains and mash them on a mortar. Add some mashed garlic and pieces of chicharron.
  4. Once you have mashed all plantains, mold them into the shape of half sphere using your hands overa container. Serve hot with chicken broth or your favorite meat.
